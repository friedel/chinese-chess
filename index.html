How to Play Chinese Chess

Basic Rules:

  • The game is played between two players: Red (starts) and Black
  • Each piece has its own movement pattern
  • The goal is to checkmate the opponent's General (similar to King in chess)

Pieces and Movements:

  • General (将/帅): Moves one step horizontally or vertically within the palace
  • Advisor (士/仕): Moves one step diagonally within the palace
  • Elephant (象/相): Moves exactly two steps diagonally
  • Horse (马): Moves one step orthogonally then one step diagonally outward
  • Chariot (车): Moves any number of steps horizontally or vertically
  • Cannon (炮): Moves like Chariot, but captures by jumping over exactly one piece
  • Soldier (兵/卒): Moves one step forward before crossing river; after crossing can move horizontally too
